## User story

**As a** site administrator
**I want to** cancel a clinic series
**So that** recurring availability can be removed when it is no longer needed.

## Acceptance criteria

- **Given** I cancel a clinic series with affected bookings
  **When** I continue
  **Then** I must choose whether to keep or cancel those bookings.

- **Given** I review the cancellation
  **When** I confirm
  **Then** the whole series is removed from the clinics list.
