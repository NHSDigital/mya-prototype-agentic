## Why this journey exists

Clinic series need to change as site capacity and service plans change. This journey edits the
whole recurring clinic object, including its date range, weekdays, times, services and closures.

## Key decisions

- The summary page is the hub, so users can change one property without walking a wizard.
- Series-only fields stay in this concept journey instead of being hidden inside variants.
- Changes that affect existing bookings surface an explicit affected-bookings step before saving.
