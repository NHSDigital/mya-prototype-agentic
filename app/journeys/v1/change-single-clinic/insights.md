## Why this journey exists

Standalone single clinics can move or change without affecting a recurring pattern. This journey
keeps that concept separate from series editing so the map shows only the relevant screens.

## Key decisions

- The summary page is the hub, so users can change one property without walking a wizard.
- Single clinics include date, times and services, but not weekdays or closures.
- Changes that affect existing bookings surface an explicit affected-bookings step before saving.
